COOKING FISH ROLEX(CHAPATI AND EGG DISH RECIPE)

INGREDIENTS USED TO MAKE ROLEX

  1. all purpose floor
  2. half tablespoon salt
  3. 1 tbsp vegetable oil or melted butter (optional, for softer chapatis)
  4. 3/4 cup warm water (adjust as needed)
  5. 1 tsp baking powder (optional, for fluffier chapati)
  6. 2-3 eggs (depending on preference)
  7. 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  8. 1-2 tomatoes, diced
  9. 1/2 tsp ground cumin or paprika (optional, for flavor)
  10. Oil for frying (vegetable or any preferred oil)
  11. Sliced avocado (for extra creaminess)
  12. Lettuce or other greens for crunch

    13. INSTURUCTIONS

    1. Mix Dough: In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Gradually add water while mixing until a soft dough forms. Knead for about 5–7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    2. Rest the Dough: Cover with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
    3. Divide the dough into small balls (about the size of a golf ball).
    4. Roll each ball into a thin circle (about 8 inches in diameter).
    5. Heat a pan or skillet over medium heat. Lightly oil the surface and cook each chapati for about 1–2 minutes on each side until golden brown spots appear.
    6. Whisk Eggs: In a bowl, whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
    7. Lay a chapati flat on a plate. Place the cooked omelet on top of the chapati. Roll the chapati tightly around the omelet like a wrap Slice in half (if desired) and serve warm.
